RadiusDamage( <origin>, <range>, <max damage>, <min damage>, [attacker] )

Module: Damage

Summary:

Does damage to all damageable objects within a given radius. The amount of damage is linear according to how close the object is to the radius. See also Entity/radiusdamage to specify an entity the damage is coming from.



Example:

RadiusDamage( level.player.origin, 500, max, min, attacker );



Required Args:

  • 1 : <origin> The centre of the damage.
  • 2 : <range> The radius of the damage done.
  • 3 : <max damage> The maximum damage done. This will be done to objects close to the origin
  • 4 : <min damage> The minimum damage done. This will be done to objects at the edge of the damage sphere.


Optional Args

  • 1 : [attacker] The attacker. Defaults to world.
