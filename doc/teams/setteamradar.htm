SetTeamRadar( <team>, <availability> )

Module: Teams
MP Only

Summary:

Sets whether a team has radar or not



Example:

SetTeamRadar( "allies", true )



Required Args:

  • 1 : <team> The name of a team. Must be either Must be 'axis', 'allies' or 'none'.
  • 2 : <availability> Whether the team has radar; defaults to false
